Royal Skeddadle

Lead Game Designer

Level Designer

Narrative Designer

Mission Designer

Product Manager

Researcher

Dina is the young heiress of the Ganlir's kingdom throne. However she has no desire to inherit it. There is only one thing this 11 year old princess wants: To explore the world with her own two feet. However her father, Alder Ganlir, doesn't allow her to leave the palace and locks Dina in her room at night. However, one night a fairy visited her room and gifted her a magic stone that allows her to teleport to it! Now that Dina has the power to escape she will have to stealth through the patrolling guards and solving puzzles to open doors. But there are many ways to exit the palace, which one will she choose?

The overall scope of this project was large and required multiple abilities which I wasn't experienced in. The non-linear nature of the game made me have to learn new ways of design the Level. The multiple exits and different paths that could be taken by the player proved to be an interesting challenge to design balanced missions. It also allowed me to better hone my communication and leadership skills as well as learning how to do efficient research, in regards to the castle rooms and objet dimensions.

  • 3D Level Design

Unrealeased Game Reviews

You are walking the streets of Victorian England, on your way back from work. The run-down building you pass every day has finally been renovated and the sign outside reads “Hildred’s Tavern”. Feeling tired from a hard day’s work, you decide to go in and are met by a friendly barkeep who gladly serves you a hot meat pie and a refreshing ale. As you leave, you notice the sign for the shop above the tavern, “Percival’s Tailor Shop”. You could use a new suit, so you decide to make a visit. The tailor greets you and leads you into the back room. As he sizes you up, he thrusts his knife in your neck. The last words you hear are “You’ll make a nice pie.”

This is the reality that the Hither Blue Lake’s citizens face in Ravenous Devils. In this flavourful Sweeney Todd inspired game which is Bad Vice Studios third and newest project, you play as Hildred and Percival, a murderous couple who are on the run after being chased out of their previous town. They’ve set-up their tavern in an abandoned building, which used to be a barber shop. Now, it’s your job to make the business thrive with the help of a secret Ingredient: Crime! A little murder never hurt anyone, if we ignore the people being killed. You can use the meat for the pies and fix up their clothes to sell. A resourceful business is a profitable one.
